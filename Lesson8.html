Grammar: Adjective Types

Na-adjectives: Use なな before nouns

Shizuka na heyaしずかなへや → A quiet room

I-adjectives: End with いい and directly modify nouns

Ōkii ieおおきいいえ → A big house

Negative forms:

Na-adj: Shizuka ja arimasenしずかじゃありません → Not quiet

I-adj: Ōkikunai desuおおきくないです → Not big

Translation Activity 8

Example Sentences

1. Cherry blossoms are beautiful.

Sakura wa kirei desu.さくらはきれいです

2. Mt. Fuji is high.

Fuji-san wa takai desu.ふじさんはたかいです

3. Is Osaka lively?

Ōsaka wa nigiyaka desu ka?おおさかはにぎやかですか

→ Yes, it is. Hai, nigiyaka desu.はい、にぎやかです

4. Is it cold in Beijing now?

Pekin wa ima samui desu ka?ペキンはいまさむいですか

→ Yes, it is very cold. Hai, totemo samui desu.はい、とてもさむいです

5. Is it cold in Shanghai too?

Shanhai mo samui desu ka?シャンハイもさむいですか

→ No, it is not so cold. Iie, amari samukunai desu.いいえ、あまりさむくないです

6. How do you like the town?

Machi wa dō desu ka?まちはどうですか

→ It is clean and convenient. Kirei de benri desu.きれいでべんりです

7. Which is Mr. Ibarra's umbrella?

Ibarra-san no kasa wa dore desu ka?イバラさんのかさはどれですか

→ That black one is. Ano kuroi kasa desu.あのくろいかさです

Conversation: "It's almost time to leave"

Rai Tanabi:

Have you got accustomed to living in Japan, Naraya?

Naraya-san, Nihon no seikatsu ni naremashita ka?ナラヤさん、にほんのせいかつになれましたか

Naraya Ramos:

Yes, I am. I enjoy it every day.

Hai, mainichi tanoshii desu.はい、まいにちたのしいです

Rai Tanabi:

Really? Mr. Ibarra, how is your work?

Sō desu ka. Ibarra-san, o-shigoto wa dō desu ka?そうですか。イバラさん、おしごとはどうですか

Dante Ibarra:

Well, it's busy, but interesting.

Sō desu ne, isogashii desu ga, omoshiroi desu.そうですね、いそがしいですが、おもしろいです

Ichiro Tanabi:

Would you like another cup of coffee?

Kōhii o mō ippai ikaga desu ka?コーヒーをもういっぱいいかがですか

Naraya Ramos:

No, thank you.

Iie, kekkō desu.いいえ、けっこうです

Dante Ibarra:

Oh, it's 8 o'clock now. We must be going.

A, mō hachi-ji desu. Sorosoro shitsurei shimasu.あ、もうはちじです。そろそろしつれいします

Rai Tanabi:

Oh, is it?

Sō desu ka?そうですか

Naraya Ramos:

Thank you for everything today.

Kyō wa arigatō gozaimashita.きょうはありがとうございました

Ichiro Tanabi:

Our pleasure. Please come again.

Iie, mata irasshatte kudasai.いいえ、またいらっしゃってください
