Grammar: Sentence Pattern

N1 wa N2 (place) desu. → "N1 is in/at N2."

Kyōshitsu wa asoko desu.きょうしつはあそこです

→ The classroom is there.

Shokudō wa 8-kai desu.しょくどうははちかいです

→ The dining hall is on the 8th floor.

Grammar: Ko/So/A/Do Series

Series Thing Person Place Direction/Place (Polite)

O-kuni → (Polite) "country"

→ Where are you from?

Grammar: Adjective Types

Na-adjectives: Use なな before nouns

Shizuka na heyaしずかなへや → A quiet room

I-adjectives: End with いい and directly modify nouns

Ōkii ieおおきいいえ → A big house

Negative forms:

Na-adj: Shizuka ja arimasenしずかじゃありません → Not quiet

I-adj: Ōkikunai desuおおきくないです → Not big

Translation Activity 3

Sentence Patterns

1. This is a dining hall.

Koko wa shokudō desu.ここはしょくどうです

2. The telephone is over there.

Denwa wa asoko desu.でんわはあそこです

Example Sentences

1. Is this Shin-Osaka?

Koko wa Shin-Ōsaka desu ka?ここはしんおおさかですか

→ Yes, it is. Hai, sō desu.はい、そうです

2. Where is the robii?

Robii wa doko desu ka?ロビーはどこですか

→ It is over there. Asoko desu.あそこです

3. Where is Mr. Tomoyo?

Tomoyo-san wa doko desu ka?ともよさんはどこですか

→ He is in the office. Jimusho desu.じむしょです

4. Where is the escalator?

Esukarētā wa doko desu ka?エスカレーターはどこですか

→ It is there. Soko desu.そこです

5. Which country are you from?

O-kuni wa dochira desu ka?おくにはどちらですか

→ Philippines. Firipin desu.フィリピンです

6. Where are those shoes from?

Sono kutsu wa doko no desu ka?そのくつはどこのですか

→ They're Philippine shoes. Firipin no kutsu desu.フィリピンのくつです

7. How much is this watch?

Kono tokei wa ikura desu ka?このとけいはいくらですか

→ It's 15,600 yen. Ichiman gosen roppyaku en desu.いちまんごせんろっぴゃくえんです

Exercise B: Conversation (Shopping) - "I'll take it"

Narayama:

Excuse me. Where is the wine department?

Sumimasen. Wain uriba wa doko desu ka?すみません。ワインうりばはどこですか

Sales clerk A:

It is in the first basement.

Chika ikkai desu.ちかいっかいです

Narayama:

Thanks.

Arigatō gozaimasu.ありがとうございます

Narayama:

Excuse me. Could you show me that wine?

Sumimasen. Sono wain o misete kudasai.すみません。そのワインをみせてください

Sales clerk B:

Of course, here it is.

Hai, dōzo.はい、どうぞ

Narayama:

Is this French wine?

Kore wa Furansu no wain desu ka?これはフランスのワインですか

Sales clerk B:

Yes, it is.

Hai, sō desu.はい、そうです

Narayama:

How much is it?

Ikura desu ka?いくらですか

Sales clerk B:

2,500 yen.

Nisen gohyaku en desu.にせんごひゃくえんです

Narayama:

Well, I'll take it.

Ja, kore o kudasai.じゃ、これをください

Translation Activity 8

Example Sentences

1. Cherry blossoms are beautiful.

Sakura wa kirei desu.さくらはきれいです

2. Mt. Fuji is high.

Fuji-san wa takai desu.ふじさんはたかいです

3. Is Osaka lively?

Ōsaka wa nigiyaka desu ka?おおさかはにぎやかですか

→ Yes, it is. Hai, nigiyaka desu.はい、にぎやかです

4. Is it cold in Beijing now?

Pekin wa ima samui desu ka?ペキンはいまさむいですか

→ Yes, it is very cold. Hai, totemo samui desu.はい、とてもさむいです

5. Is it cold in Shanghai too?

Shanhai mo samui desu ka?シャンハイもさむいですか

→ No, it is not so cold. Iie, amari samukunai desu.いいえ、あまりさむくないです

6. How do you like the town?

Machi wa dō desu ka?まちはどうですか

→ It is clean and convenient. Kirei de benri desu.きれいでべんりです

7. Which is Mr. Ibarra's umbrella?

Ibarra-san no kasa wa dore desu ka?イバラさんのかさはどれですか

→ That black one is. Ano kuroi kasa desu.あのくろいかさです
